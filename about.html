What is FriendComp?

Established in 2011, FriendComp is a platform created by young savvy marketing people seeking to pump some excitement into social media and find a real solution for retailers and small businesses to drive foot traffic, increase word of mouth referrals and generate more revenue!

FriendComp enhances the social media experience by enabling people to gift real cash, otherwise known as “comps” to their friends for redemption at one of our participating FriendComp merchants.

FriendComp is a pioneer in bringing gifting to users of social media. No longer will communities like Facebook and Twitter be just a place to verbally express your friendship, with FriendComp you can now put your money where your heart is and show your network of friends how generous you are!

Our Mission

FriendComp’s mission is to enable people who engage in social media to gift one another with items that lead to a next future social experience and enable merchants to generate referrals to their business and create awareness for them in social media.

Our Values

FriendComp is committed to supporting local businesses, the economic glue of our cities and ultimately our nations. We believe firmly in theory that you “get what you put out there” and are dedicated to spreading generosity around the world.
