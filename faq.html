FAQ

  • What is FriendComp?

    FriendComp is a social gift-giving service. It lets you give Paid Gift Credits to friends and family using your smartphone.

    It’s fast, easy and fun. Best of all, many of FriendComp’s partners offer extra value for folks paying with FriendComps.

  • How do I get started?

    By signing up for a FriendComp account via the website or simply by downloading the mobile app. It’s fast, free and easy.

    Connect with Facebook to make login and sending comps even easier!

  • How do I send a FriendComp?

    Open up your FriendComp application Find a friend or family member to celebrate, and select that person Pick the amount of credits you want to give, write a greeting and enter payment details ( After your first purchase you will be set up to keep buying comps with your preferred credit card number.)

    Press the Give button and you’re done! Your friend will be notified of your gift via email, Facebook or text message—it’s up to you. Our app also notifies existing users of each gift credit they receive, as well as any contributions.

  • Who can see the amount I add to a friend’s gift credit?

    If the gift credit has already been delivered, your friend will see the exact amount you have contributed to the value of the original gift credit. Mutual friends and other contributors will see only the personal message and the name of the merchant the gift credit was given towards.

  • Do I need a smartphone to send gifts with FriendComp?

    No. You can send FriendComp Gift Credits using other mobile devices such as an iPad. You can also borrow a friend’s smart phone, download the application and log into your Facebook account. You will have to log out when you have finished.

  • Can I cancel a paid FriendComp gift credit and get my money back?

    It depends on the situation. If you gave a paid gift credit to a friend who has used the card, no refunds are allowed. We can cancel the purchase if it is under 14 days since you sent It - if the gift credit is unused. You can request a refund by contacting customer support by email: support@FriendComp.com.

  • Is it safe to buy FriendComp Gift Credits?

    Yes. We take your security seriously. At no time is your credit card or PayPal information ever stored on our servers. Sensitive payment details are encrypted and transmitted to a third-party payment services provider that operates under strict Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards (PCI-DSS).

  • Do I need a FriendComp account to redeem a FriendComp I received from a friend?

    Yes. Signing up for a FriendComp account is fast and easy. Visit the Sign Up Page and register an account. Or simply click on the link in the emailed FriendComp you received to redeem it.

    The easiest option is to simply download our app from the App Store.

  • Do I need a smartphone to use my FriendComp gift credit?

    The Gift Credits that must be redeemed in person at a business require the use of the FriendComp mobile app on an iOS or Android smartphone or mobile device. These include the iPhone, iPad with 3G – Android versions are in the works.

  • How do I redeem my gift credit at a business?

    Using your mobile device, either open the link you received via email, facebook, or by downloading and opening the app.

    Pull up the gift credit you want to redeem by going to “My Comps”.

    When you are ready to redeem the coupon, Press the “REDEEM” button. You will only have a limited time to show the merchant your comp so do not redeem until you are certain that you will be redeeming your comp.

    Your gift credit and its unique code will be displayed on the screen. Present your smartphone to the cashier, who will confirm the FriendComp and apply your discount or credit to your transaction.

  • Can I print my gift credit to use it?

    No. This service is purely digital, which makes it great for the planet

  • When can I use my gift credit?

    Immediately! You can start shopping as soon as the gift credit is delivered and you downloadn the application!

  • For how long is my gift credit valid?

    It varies depending on state laws – Typically it will be 30 days.

  • Can I use several FriendComp Gift Credits at the same time?

    Yes, it’s just like cash, so go for it!

  • What happens if my gift credit expires?

    An expired gift credit is no longer valid. It is automatically removed from your account. But don´t worry, we will remind you to use your gift credit before it expires.

  • My gift credit disappeared before I could use it. What’s wrong?

    A gift credit is removed from your FriendComp account for one of four reasons:

    1. It has already been redeemed for a product or service.

    2. The card has expired and is no longer valid for use.

    The friend who sent you a paid gift credit has cancelled the purchase. (Sorry!)

    Your account is suspected of potentially violating the terms of use. Contact customer support for more information.

  • I don't have a smartphone. How can I redeem a FriendComp?

    The easiest solution is to borrow a friend's smartphone, access your email, and click on the link to redeem.

  • What if the merchant for my FriendComp gift credit goes out of business?

    If you are unable to redeem a valid, paid gift credit, we’ll make sure you get your money back. Contact customer support at support@FriendComp.com and explain your situation.

  • How can I get my business on FriendComp?

    If you are interested in partnering with FriendComp to increase traffic to your business, please fill out the form on the merchant sign up page to get more information.
